Refund Policy

Our refund policy is as follows:

  • If you cancel your order within 24 hours, you will receive a full refund.
  • If you cancel your order after 24 hours, you will receive a partial refund (50% of the total order amount).
  • No refunds will be issued after the order has been dispatched.
  • If you receive a damaged or incorrect item, please contact us within 48 hours for a replacement or a full refund.

For any other questions, please contact our customer service.
